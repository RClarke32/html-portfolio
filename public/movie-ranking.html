Christian's Best Movies

Top four favorite movies of all time.

Django Unchained

Django is a Quentin Tarantino action western. The story line and action scenes are very exciting to watch

Friday Night Lights

Friday night lights is a true story about a high school football team in Permian Texas. I love football and relate to alot of the story in this movie.

Black Panther

Black Panther is a part of the Marvel Series. Two of my favorite actors are Chadwick Boseman and Michael B. Jordan. I am a big fan of Marvel and like the story line and action in this movie.

Without Remorse

Military movie. Revenge movie with a sweet ending. A lot of action and fighting.
